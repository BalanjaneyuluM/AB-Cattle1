WHAT MAKES OUR MILK SO SPECIAL?

At AB Cattle Inc., our journey begins with the joy of contented cows, and it extends through
meticulous care at every stage of our processes. We firmly believe in the mantra that happy cows yield exceptional milk.
Did you know that cows thrive on a predictable, uneventful lifestyle? Without it, they can become disoriented,
uneasy, and, in essence, stressed. A stressed cow's milk production decreases, and the taste is notably affected. It's true!

Furthermore, the key to a happy cow lies in its good health. At AB Cattle.inc, our cows are nurtured by our devoted farmers and veterinarians
who ensure an ideal living environment for our cherished herd. We prioritize their well-being to maintain
their happiness and, consequently, the superior quality of our products.

THE MILKING PROCESS

At AB Cattle Inc., tending to our 2,800 Holstein cows is an organized and routine-driven endeavor, recognizing the value cows place on consistency.
The process commences as we open the gates, prompting the cows to gracefully navigate down the alley toward our cutting-edge milking carousel.
This carousel, boasting 72 stalls, revolves at a deliberate and steady pace. Upon entering,
each cow undergoes a thorough cleansing of her teats and udders before being seamlessly connected to an
automated milking unit. The unit disengages once the cow completes the milking process,
which takes just under 10 minutes, ensuring an efficient rotation as the cows depart individually to return to their designated pens.
