    WHO WE ARE

    AB Cattle, Inc. is situated in Farmington, CT, proudly holding the title of the largest dairy farm in the northeast! With roots dating back to the late 1890s,
    our commitment to advancing farming practices spans over five generations. We firmly believe that understanding your farms is synonymous with knowing your food.
    Thus, we champion transparency and extend an open invitation to our friends and community to join our extended dairy family.

    At AB Cattle, Inc., our mission is straightforward yet impactful: to forge a better future for families by producing safe and wholesome food while creating
    value for our employees, owners, and the community we serve. We advocate for transparency and warmly invite you to witness our operations firsthand. Feel free
    to click the button on the left to schedule a tour or explore a captivating video created by our exceptional team by clicking the
    "Magic of AB Cattle" button on the right.

    IT'S MORE THEN JUST COW'S & MILK

    Our journey starts with contented cows, and this sense of well-being permeates every stage of our meticulous processes. When we declare
    that contented cows yield superior milk, it's more than just a statement! Did you know that cows thrive in a calm and predictable environment?
    A lack of routine can leave them bewildered and, in essence, "stressed out.

    A contented cow is a productive cow, and you can genuinely discern the difference in the milk quality.Yes, it's true! Another key to a cow's
    happiness is good health. At Oakridge, our devoted farmers and veterinarians ensure an optimal living environment for our beloved cows,
    fostering their well-being and contentment.

    OUR ROOTS

    It all started over 100 years ago with the Adolph Bahler family. Our family was one of many small farms scattered across New England raising
    not only dairy cows but also growing tobacco and potatoes as cash crops. Through the decades dairy became our passion and while time moved
    on and other dairy farms closed their doors, we pushed forward. Through that process we learned one thing; quality does not happen by chance;
    it’s done on purpose. In 2016 we decided to take things to the next level by building our current facility that is 318,000 sq. ft. We built this
    with three things in mind: first and foremost the comfort of our cows and efficiency & sustainability. The integrity of our farm and milk has been
    our priority from the beginning.

    OUR FUTURE

    Now well into a 5th generation, our goal is to get great tasting milk from our parlor to your home as quickly as possible so you’re drinking
    fresh quality milk that you can trust. 50 years ago your local milkman would deliver fresh milk to your doorstep. Every family knew
    the farm it came from. And you knew it was fresh. Thus began, The Modern Milkman, where old fashioned farming and service meets today’s day and age.
    The Modern Milkman is a weekly subscription-based box service that delivers fresh AB Cattle.INC> (milked less than 24 hours ago) along with
    local farm fresh eggs, butter, yogurt, cheese, and so many other products right to your doorstep. While a lot of our milk is still being supplied to
    Guida’s Milk in New Britain, CT our heart is in The Modern Milkman and believe this is the future of dairy, where community and honest dairy come together.
