LUXURIOUS LIVING ACCOMMADATIONS.



At AB Cattle Inc., we boast a rich legacy of prioritizing exceptional animal care, fostering a healthy, secure,
and nurturing environment for our beloved herd. With approximately 2800 Holstein cows in our care,
comprising around 2400 actively milking and 400 in a dry state, we have meticulously crafted a haven
for them to leisurely spend their days amidst their companions.


A tour of our facilities will reveal our expansive open-stall barn, spanning 8 acres, designed to allow cows the freedom
to roam at their leisure for activities such as eating, drinking, resting, and socializing. This barn
layout mirrors an outdoor setting, providing the cows with a comfortable retreat shielded from the
elements—offering shade during the summer months and warmth throughout the winter. To further enhance their
well-being, we maintain 2415 open stalls, ensuring ample space and natural ventilation.
Rest assured, at AB Cattle Inc., our commitment to providing our cows with abundant space and a conducive
environment is unwavering.

THE INPUT OF QUALITY RESULT IN QUALITY OUTPUT.

At AB Cattle Inc., our cows are nourished with feed cultivated, grown, and harvested on our premises. To optimize nutritional
care,the cows are strategically grouped based on milk production, and a professional nutritionist
tailors a special recipe consisting of a blend of hay, grains,proteins, vitamins, and minerals.
Water, an essential component of their diet, is readily available to our cows throughout the day,
sourced from clean and well-maintained sources.

Within the barn, our diligent team ensures that food is consistently provided to the cows, with any remaining feed removed and replaced
with fresh batches daily. Additionally, our daily routine involves the meticulous removal of
manure from the stalls,conducted when the cows are away for milking, and we prioritize the
addition of fresh, comfortable bedding to enhance their overall comfort and well-being.

OUR AFFECTION FOR OUR COWS IS UNWAVREING!

Above all, we are farmers with a deep love for our cows at AB Cattle Inc. Our utmost priority is ensuring the thriving well-being of our cows, and
we spare no effort to make it a reality. At our facilities, we have a specialized veterinary area where every aspect of our cows is diligently
monitored, including their birthdays, vaccination records, parentage, and milk production. Every detail is observed to ensure nothing goes unnoticed.
For added care, we have designated spaces for tasks like hoof trimming, akin to a "cow pedicure," scheduled a few times each year.
Our committed medical team, consisting of skilled technicians and certified veterinarians from AB Cattle Inc.,
is consistently present to provide our cows with preventive care and immediate medical attention, ensuring their
overall health and happiness.







OUR NURSERY

AB Cattle Inc. features a dedicated medical section focused on providing personalized care and maternity support for our herd.
Following the birthing process, newborns receive their initial nourishment from their mothers, consuming colostrum—rich in
antibodies crucial for combating disease-causing bacteria and viruses. Our female calves, or heifers, reside in our
specialized young stock facility until they reach approximately 6 months of age, ensuring their optimal development. Meanwhile, young males,
or bulls, undergo their upbringing in western facilities. Post their time in the young stock facility, they are raised until
reaching the age of 2 when they begin contributing to milk production.
On an annual basis, AB Cattle Inc. welcomes around 2000 calves, with a substantial portion—ranging
from 1200 to 1300—comprising female calves.
